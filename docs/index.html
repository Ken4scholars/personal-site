About Me

I am an ML engineer, software engineer and programming mentor. I got my Computer Science degree from Innopolis University, then proceeded to get a Master degree in Artificial Intelligence from The National University of Singapore. I have experience working with companies across different fields and technology stacks ranging from development of scalable APIs to distributed GIS infrastructure. My major interests include Machine Learning/Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing and Backend Software Development. The technologies I have used in the course of my work and study include Python, Java, Scala, Pytorch, Scikit Learn, Keras, Django etc. Here I sometimes talk about tech, my work and life in general. You’re welcome to my little world :)
